Hi! My name is Curt Cato. I am a Web Developer, and before beginning my career in software development, I had a career in the commercial construction industry. I worked as a superintendent, supervising and managing multiple projects. After about a decade in the construction industry, I decided to make a change. I decided to enter the technology industry as a developer; something I had been contemplating for several years. This was one of the hardest decisions I have ever made, and I would not take it back for anything! The experience of learning software at Nashville Software School has been incredibly rewarding and challenging. I have always felt hardwired to think logically, so I feel as though this field is exactly right for me. I look forward to solving problems as a Software Developer for the rest of my career.
